# Privacy Policy for SketchOff

**Last updated: January 6, 2026**

## Overview

SketchOff ("we", "our", or "the app") is a multiplayer drawing game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Display Name**: When you join a game, you enter a nickname. This is only visible to other players in your game room and is not permanently stored.
- **Drawings**: Your drawings are temporarily stored to enable gameplay and are automatically deleted when the game ends.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Anonymous Identifier**: We use Firebase Authentication to create an anonymous session. This does not collect your email, name, or any personal information.
- **Game Statistics**: Your personal game stats (games played, scores) are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

- Enable multiplayer gameplay between you and other players
- Display drawings during the game's rating phase
- Track your personal game statistics on your device

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Collect personal identifying information
- Track your location

## Data Retention

- **Game data** (room info, drawings, player names): Automatically deleted within 1-24 hours after the game ends
- **Local statistics**: Stored on your device until you delete the app

## Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (by Google) for:

- Anonymous authentication
- Temporary data storage during gameplay
- Image storage for drawings

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Children's Privacy

SketchOff does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal information to play.

## Photo Library Access

If you choose to save a drawing to your device, the app will request access to your photo library. This permission is optional and only used to save images you explicitly choose to save.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
