# Privacy Policy for SketchOff

**Last updated: January 11, 2026**

## Overview

SketchOff ("we", "our", or "the app") is a multiplayer drawing game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Consent

By using SketchOff's multiplayer features, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. When you first open the app, you will be asked to review and accept this privacy policy before using multiplayer features.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Display Name**: When you join a multiplayer game, you enter a nickname. This is uploaded to our servers to display to other players in your game room. It is automatically deleted when the game ends.
- **Drawings**: Your drawings are uploaded to our servers to enable other players to view and rate them during gameplay.
- **Ratings**: During multiplayer games, the scores you give to other players' drawings are uploaded to calculate game results.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Anonymous Identifier**: We use Firebase Authentication to create an anonymous session. This does not collect your email, real name, or any personal identifying information.
- **Game Statistics**: Your personal game stats (games played, wins, scores) are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

- Enable multiplayer gameplay between you and other players
- Display your drawings to other players during the rating phase
- Calculate and display game scores within your game room
- Track your personal game statistics locally on your device

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Collect personal identifying information
- Track your location

## Data Retention

- **Game room data** (room info, player names, scores): Deleted after the game session ends
- **Drawings**: Stored temporarily to enable gameplay; may be retained for a limited period after the game ends
- **Local statistics**: Stored on your device until you delete the app

## Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (by Google) for:

- Anonymous authentication
- Temporary data storage during gameplay
- Image storage for drawings

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Children's Privacy

SketchOff does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal information to play.

## Photo Library Access

If you choose to save a drawing to your device, the app will request access to your photo library. This permission is optional and only used to save images you explicitly choose to save.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
